About

2020, a year experienced ever. Things will change and they have to change. From now on, the sound of nation will experience the next era of recording, sound design and solutions.

Mix Room was created, crafted and designed for Nepalese Music Fraternity to experience a next level of Music and Audio production. Our new facility boasts studios for recording, production, analogue and hybrid mixing suites, mastering suites, advance post-production, acoustics solutions and businesses, an academy for people to learn Music Production/ Audio Production and showcasing some cutting edge technologies of world’s music industry.

Pratik Thapa, CEO at Mix Room Studios Nepal, an Audio Engineer from SAE Qantm- Sydney, Australia, Electronic Music Producer from SAE Institute and AAT Media College- Bengaluru, India and officially certified Pro-Tools user from Avid, USA.

Mix Room Studios has wider network in various recording studios at different countries and have also assigned various international producers and engineers for studio’s projects. Our clients can take fullest advantage of our wider network for recording and producing their music and sounds.

We believe in Arts. We believe in Education. We believe in Humanity.
